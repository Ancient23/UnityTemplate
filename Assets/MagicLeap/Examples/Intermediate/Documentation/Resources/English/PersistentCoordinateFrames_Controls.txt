<color=#dbfb76><b>Hover</b></color>
- Hovering over a ball will highlight it.
- When the visualizer is active, hovering also shows a line from the ball to the PCF it is bound to.

<color=#dbfb76><b>Hometap</b></color>
- Toggles the PCF Visualizer.

<color=#dbfb76><b>Bumper</b></color>
- Creates a persistent ball that automatically binds to a pcf and saves to the persistent store.

<color=#dbfb76><b>Touchpad</b></color>
- Tapping on a ball will remove it and remove it's pcf binding.
- Performing a radial scroll will remove all content and delete the local JSON file.

<color=#dbfb76><b>Trigger</b></color>
- Hold the trigger on a ball and move the control to change the ball's position.
